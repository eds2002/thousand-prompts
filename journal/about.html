About Us

What is Thousand Prompts

The idea

Thousand Prompts is a simple web app / phone app that allows you to journal with the addition of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy.

How do you use Thousand Prompts?

Settings

There's not much to offer in settings, we're fairly new. Simply click the settings icon at the top of your screen to:

1) Disable CBT
2) Turn on dark mode
3) Reset your prompts

Creating your account

Creating your account is fast and secure! You can either create a account with email and password, or create an account with google.

Using without an account

You don't need an account! But... sadly we can only offer you 30 prompts. Unfortunately, local storage is limited in how much data can be stored, and it's not much! If you create an account, you can access all of our available prompts as of now.

Picking prompts

We have a variety of prompts with different goals! You can select prompts that make you feel good, prompts that require heavy thinking, or prompts that require you to be grateful.

Distortions & Current Mood

While it's not required you select distortions, it could help you. This is the first step to Cognitive Behavioral Therapy. Select your mood, if you're upset, a list of distortions is displayed. Acknowledging what distortions you're facing at the moment can help aleviate those current thoughts and eliminate them for the present moment.

Capturing your mood after

This step isn't necessary either, but is the last step in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy. If you're mood is postive, add onto it! It helps to write your positive moments down. If you're mood is negative, pretend you're going to comfort a friend, or a family member. Treat yourself kindly, and cheer yourself up!
